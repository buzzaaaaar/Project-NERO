Shipping

Thank you for choosing Nero Beauty for your self-care needs! To ensure clarity and transparency, please review our shipping policy.

Processing Time:
Orders are typically shipped islandwide within 2-4 business days after payment confirmation, excluding weekends and holidays.

Shipping Rates and Delivery Times:
We offer standard shipping options during checkout. Standard shipping within Sri Lanka generally takes 5-7 business days for delivery. Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight, size, and destination of the package. You can view the shipping costs at checkout before finalizing your purchase.

Address Accuracy:
Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping information. Please double-check your shipping address during checkout to ensure timely and accurate delivery. We cannot be held responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

Delivery Issues:
In the event of delivery delays or issues, please contact our customer service team at +94 555 1234. We will do our best to assist and resolve any problems regarding your shipment.

Shipping Holidays:
Please note that shipping carriers may observe holidays, which can affect the delivery schedule. Expect delays during peak holiday seasons.

Returns Due to Shipping Issues:
If your order is returned to us due to an incorrect address provided by the customer or delivery failure after multiple attempts, a reshipping fee may be applied. Please ensure accurate and complete shipping information to avoid such instances.










Returns And Exchanges

At Nero Beauty, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please take a moment to review our returns and exchanges policy.

Eligibility for Returns and Exchanges:
We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days from the date of purchase. To be eligible, items must be unused, in the same condition as received, and in the original packaging.

Non-Returnable and Non-Exchangeable Items:
For hygiene and safety reasons, opened or used products cannot be returned or exchanged unless they are found to be damaged or defective upon arrival.

Initiating a Return or Exchange:
To start a return or exchange process, please contact our customer service team at +94 555 1234. Provide your order number, details of the item(s) you wish to return or exchange, and the reason for the return or exchange request. Our team will guide you through the process and provide instructions for returning the item(s).

Processing Time:
Please allow 3-4 business days for us to process your exchange once we receive the item(s).

Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Items:
If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect skincare product, please contact us immediately upon receipt. We will arrange a replacement at no additional cost.

Return Shipping:
Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item received is damaged, defective, or incorrect.

No Refunds:
Please note that we do not offer refunds for skincare products, only exchanges for unopened and unused items as outlined in our policy.
